# GEO / Agentic SEO Notes

## Core idea

Search in 2026 is increasingly mediated by AI systems. Lewkai should optimize not only for rankings but for retrievability, citability, and trust in AI-generated responses.

## Important ideas

- content should be semantically complete and machine-readable
- AI systems reward factual density and originality
- multimodal content can increase citation likelihood
- authority and first-hand experience matter heavily
- proprietary insight is a competitive moat

## Practical implementation directions

- use schema/JSON-LD thoroughly
- consider `llms.txt`
- publish self-contained high-signal content blocks
- create strong author/entity identity
- track AI-specific visibility metrics, not only classic rankings

## Lewkai-specific advantage

Lewkai can differentiate through:
- first-principles AI implementation thinking
- the articulation problem framing
- practical agent/system architecture experience
- local + international positioning with compliance-aware messaging where relevant
