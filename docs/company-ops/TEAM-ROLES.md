# Team Roles

## Recommendation

**Set the structure first, then fill in the details with Dom.**

Reason:
- a simple structure helps everyone think clearly
- you do not need perfect information to create the headings
- Dom can then react to something concrete instead of a blank page

So for now, create the role model with placeholders.

---

## Why this matters

For a consulting company with a 10-person team and partners, agents need to know:
- who owns what
- who can approve what
- who is advisory vs accountable
- who can see which client context

Without this, agent work and human work will get messy quickly.

---

## Suggested starter roles

Use these as the first pass.

### 1. Leadership / Decision Owner
Owns:
- strategic direction
- key approvals
- client-sensitive decisions
- budget / spend / risk approval

Questions for Dom:
- who is the final decision-maker?
- does this differ by client or project?

### 2. Delivery Lead / PM
Owns:
- project coordination
- priorities
- status tracking
- issue hygiene
- handoffs between humans and agents

Questions for Dom:
- who plays this role today?
- is it one person or per project?

### 3. Solution Architect
Owns:
- technical direction
- architecture choices
- platform/tool decisions
- technical review

Questions for Dom:
- who approves major architecture changes?
- when is architecture review required?

### 4. Builder / Operator
Owns:
- implementation
- config
- docs updates
- issue execution

Questions for Dom:
- which team members build directly?
- what can agents do without approval?

### 5. Reviewer / QA
Owns:
- quality review
- completeness checks
- risk flags
- release readiness

Questions for Dom:
- who signs off quality?
- is review separate from build?

### 6. Client Lead
Owns:
- client relationship
- expectation management
- communication routing
- client-facing updates

Questions for Dom:
- who is the client-facing owner per account?
- do agents ever draft client updates directly?

### 7. Partner / Contractor
Owns:
- specialist delivery where needed
- scoped outputs only

Questions for Dom:
- what can partners access?
- should partners work in GitHub only, or also in `_agent`?

Do **not** try to model every title in the company first.

- decision owner
- delivery lead
- architect
- builder
- reviewer
- client lead
- partner
